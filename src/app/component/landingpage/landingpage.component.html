PAY ME BACK

What is PAY ME BACK?

Pay Me Back is an expense management app that allows you and your friends to record expenses (for example, a trip to Japan) and let you know how much you owe and are owed!
Registering is completely optional, just be sure to record down the URL! However, if you do opt to register, you will then be able to link a Paypal account if you so wish, so that other users can pay you back through Paypal.
